Nurturing Students' Minds

We work one-on-one, one concept at a time, to build a foundation for long term success.


At SuprAcademy, we strongly believe that merely offering a traditional "tutoring" service does not guarantee a student's academic success.

We believe in the importance of parental involvement in their child's academic life. As such, we have structured our service in what we call the Triangle of Success.

For the academic support service that we provide to have the maximum effect, this triangle must remain intact. If any part of the triangle breaks, the academic support we provide loses its effectiveness.

Students Succeed By

  • Receives individuals, one-on-one academic support
  • Own account
    • Keeps student organized
    • Review of previous sessions to keep our Guides informed of student's latest academic work
    • Ability to upload future work (such as challenging homework questions) to achieve efficiency and safe student time
    • To-do lists
    • Snapshot of student's calendar to provide context on day's session
  • Personalized Resources page composed of internal and external resources tailored for student's individual use. Resources are compiled based on student's needs as observed by Guides
  • Receive best possible academic guidance based on individual learning needs

Guides Succeed By

  • Provides professional (Incomplete)
  • Highly qualified, well-trained and competent Guides

Parents Succeed By

  • Regular feedback in-person and online communication
  • Review student day-to-day session performance
  • Constant engagement via walk-in "Parent-days"
