Nurturing Students' Minds

We work one-on-one, one concept at a time, to build a foundation for long term success.


SuprAcademy is a place where the concepts and problems taught in day school are just the starting point. Everything we do revolves around the student’s regular academic work.

We believe that the key to helping a student excel is to focus on their unique learning style.

First we analyze each student’s current academic status to identify areas of strength and of challenge. Then we move on to address the areas of challenge and further their strenghts by providing more complex exercises and activities.

We work with students one-on-one in person or online.
